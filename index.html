Our Food

We feel fries are no longer just the sidekick to a meal, our perfect crispy golden fries have sides and toppings of their own.

Festival Specials

We specialise in crispy golden fries coated with a range of home made seasonings. We provide a range of exciting toppings and house favourites. We have something for everyone whether its something simple like cheese topped marmite glazed fries or the mighty, “Pimp Daddy.” Jumbo portion of fries with cajun seasoning, chorizo, pancetta, crispy onions, double cheese sauce, cheddar cheese, jalapeños and gherkins.
